Our Crew

James Wassenaar

James Wassenaar, the rhythm guitarist and keyboardist, brings a dynamic and versatile playing style to Wolfmother. His contributions to the band's sound extend beyond his instrumental skills, as he also provides backing vocals and adds layers of texture to their music. Wassenaar's ability to seamlessly transition between guitar and keyboard allows him to create rich and engaging soundscapes that complement Stockdale's lead guitar work.

Andrew Stockdale

Andrew Stockdale, the visionary frontman and guitarist of Wolfmother, is the band's creative core. His distinctive vocals and blues-infused guitar riffs have defined the band's sound since their inception in 2004. Stockdale's songwriting prowess has produced iconic tracks like "Woman," "Joker and the Thief," and "Dimensions." Beyond his musical talent, he is known for his captivating stage presence and dedication to crafting raw and energetic rock music.

Christian Condon

Christian Condon, the powerhouse drummer of Wolfmother, provides the driving force behind the band's energetic performances. His powerful and precise drumming style drives the band's songs and creates a solid foundation for their music. Condon's ability to seamlessly switch between intricate rhythms and heavy-hitting beats adds a dynamic and captivating element to Wolfmother's live shows.
